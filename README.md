# C-Suite-Health

### Closed-loop referral coordination for healthcare systems.

> Track every patient referral from submission to outcome — in one shared workflow.

---

## The Problem

Referral coordination is still held together by fax, phone calls, and manual follow-up.

Low-income patients are referred to specialty care through fragmented systems with little visibility across organizations.

The result:
- Delayed care  
- Lost referrals  
- Poor patient outcomes  

---

## The Solution

C-Suite Health brings FedEx-style tracking to patient referrals.

Primary care teams and specialists operate in one shared workflow with real-time visibility at every step.

---

## How It Works

1. Referral is submitted  
2. Specialist accepts or declines  
3. Appointment is scheduled  
4. Outcome is shared back  

All activity is tracked automatically.

---

## What This Prototype Shows

This prototype demonstrates the core closed-loop referral workflow:

- Referral intake  
- Referral status tracking  
- Specialist acceptance  
- Scheduling updates  
- Outcome visibility
